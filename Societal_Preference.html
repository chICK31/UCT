Natrual Pair of Society

I think it’s best to look at it from a Natural pair perspective. Because SJs are the natural pair for NJs, it makes it easy for NJs to live in SJ society and vice versa for SJs living in NJ society. One example of how this works is how Japan, an NJ society, is rich in tradition, which means that SJs would be able to acclimate to that environment better than any other type. And, NJs in SJ society, are enabled in SJ society by being encouraged by the SJs to perform. For instance, ENTJs could successfully start a new business, INTJs could perform in music concerts, INFJs could counsel people, and ENFJs could be a part of the education system. All within an SJ society.

Golden Pair of Society

Whereas SPs in an SJ are much like the golden pair relationship, there are challenges and enablement and vice versa for an SJ living in an SP society. In Mexico, an SP society, SJs are usually enabled and disabled depending on the specific context, but that manifests in “high highs' ' and “low lows' ' because of the oscillation between feeling wanted and useful or feeling outcast and useless. SJ to SJ society tends to be more stable as exemplified by SJ popular culture. Another example is the Mexican cartel, a fairly SP phenomena since it's an anarchy-oriented way of life. South Africa is another example of an SP society, so people often live in the moment there, whereas in Japan, an NJ society, that isn’t as much the case. Stable Pair of Society

Since the society has an understanding of its temperament, it’s able to build a society around its type, so, for SJ society, it builds the society around SJs, but it’s not technically enabling, instead, it’s merely stabilizing for the SJs in SJ society, or NJs for NJ society, etc.

Conlict Pair of Society

But for the subconscious relationship such as NPs living in SJ society (like, NPs living in the United States), it’s complete Hell. For SJs living in NP society (like, SJs living in China), it’s also complete Hell because China is a country where they’d like to have more engineers, and SJs, don’t particularly want to become engineers, whereas NPs in SJ society, SJs are trying to push religion, the affiliative school system, and boring repetitive things that aren’t conducive to how NPs get their cognitive origin.
